## Rule 1 — Respect and Communication 
All players are expected to treat each other with respect in kingdom chat, alliance chat, and private messages. Toxic behavior—including insults, harassment, discrimination, or unnecessary escalation—will not be tolerated. Disagreements should be handled calmly and constructively.
Penalty: warning, then further action (outlaw status) for repeated or severe violations

## Rule 2 — Peace Time Agreement 
We are a kingdom of peace. No attacks on others outside of COT. 
Conquest point exchanges must be mutually agreed upon between the participating members. 

Permitted Attacks —
DURING COT, all attacks are permitted. 

EXCEPTIONS. 
- Attacks on clan buildings (immediate outlaw status)
- a player over 1m might against a player under 1m might. 

Players with less than 1m can attack anyone over 100k might. 


## Rule 3 — Resource Tiles 
All Gold Mounds and Wellsprings must be registered in the appropriate chat. 
Only one mound or spring may be registered by a player in a 24 hour period. A penalty will occur if you are caught doing so. 
All mines inside a clan’s territory belong to that clan unless agreed upon and proven by written permission. 
No resource tile may be attacked regardless of event, but be mindful that other kingdoms will try and attack them. 


## RULE 4 — Portals 
Portals may be attacked during COT. 

EXCEPTIONS 
-Event portals may never be attacked. 
-Memorial portals. 

Hostile portals may be attacked

## Rule 5 — Use of Faron 
Faron may never be used during COT. Any documented use of Faron during COT will result in immediate outlaw status with SEVERE compensation to reach normal status.

## Rule 6 — Outlaws
Outlaws may be attacked regardless of events. Any clan that harbors an outlaw for more than 24 hours will result in becoming an Outlaw Clan. 
To lift outlaw status, you must pay compensation and have a judge approve satisfaction in the court to have it removed. 

## Rule 7 — Taxes. 
Taxes must be paid daily. Any excessive amount of Gold ingots will result in a warning. If a warning does not result in the taxes being paid, the palace will move to have the royal guard retrieve the Ingots. 

## Rule 8 — Compensation and Prosecution 
Any unjustified attack (outside the authorized framework) must be compensated by reimbursement of losses plus 25% within 48 hours. Refusal will result in outlaw status.
Any Gold taken will result in a fine of 3 million Silver per 1000 Gold. 

A player that is done unjustly is encouraged to handle the matter by reaching out to the offender. 
If that doesn’t work, then their clan leader. if that fails it will be reported in the violations chat where a judge will review the case and determine the outcome.

Outlaws do not reserve this right.
